World Travelers Plan Long Term for Residence

The stress of having a short term permit or one that has run out gets in the way of managing your life. So they make sure to get in a position for a B-permit, and a C-permit down the line.

Did you know that some jobs require a C-permit, and people with a C-permit must be given preference over others by law, giving you access to more jobs?
