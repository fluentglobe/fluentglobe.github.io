## Greetings and Introductions (Lesson 1)


Today I want you to learn some of the following vocabulary:

* Good Morning
* Good Afternoon
* Good Evening
* Good Night
* My name is ....
* How are you ?
* Thank You.
* Hello & Goodbye
* Nice to meet you
* Take care.
